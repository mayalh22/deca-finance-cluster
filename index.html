How it works

Built around how memory actually works

Each question you answer moves into one of three piles. The app works through your Unseen pile first, then circles back on anything you missed.

Three-pile system
Get it right and it moves to Mastered. Get it wrong and it goes to Review. The system keeps working until every question is mastered.
Explanations on every answer
Every question includes a detailed explanation so you understand the reasoning, not just the answer.
Progress saved automatically
Your mastered and review piles persist between sessions. Pick up exactly where you left off, no account required.
