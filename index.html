How it works

This is based off of what works for me:

Each question you answer moves into one of three piles. The app works through your Unseen pile first, then circles back on anything you missed.

Three-pile system
If it's right, it's mastered, wrong, it's review. You answer everything till everything is mastered
Explanations on every answer
Every question includes a detailed explanation so you understand the reasoning, rather than memorize answers.
Progress saved automatically
Your mastered and review piles persist between sessions. Pick up exactly where you left off, without an account!
